Notifiable Incident Assessment

Under the Work Health and Safety Act 2011 (Qld), some incidents must be reported to Workplace Health and Safety Queensland immediately. These are:
  • the death of a person;
  • a serious injury or illness (e.g. requiring immediate hospital admission or immediate medical treatment for specified injuries);
  • a dangerous incident (e.g. uncontrolled explosion, fire, electric shock, collapse or failure of plant/structure).
If notifiable: phone WHSQ on 1300 362 128 immediately, submit written notification within 48 hours, preserve the incident site until an inspector advises otherwise (unless to help an injured person or make the area safe), and keep the record for at least 5 years. In an emergency, call 000 first.
